Clown fish are different from other fish because they're immune to anemone stings and even live in them. And their group is led by one female only and, if the leader dies then a male turns female to take leader's place.

Beta fish also known as the Siamese fighting fish are very beautiful and have very vibrant colors. They also make for very good pets.

Flying fish are the only fish that can fly. They can do it for 15-30 seconds and in that small period of time they can glide about 200 meters!

Angelfish are different from other fish because of their unique body shape. And specially developed camoflauge adapted to their enviorment.

The blue tang is different from other fish because they start out yellow then turn blue as they mature. They can also change from blue to a darker purple or violet when scared.

The dumbo octopus is different from other octopuses because of where they live in the midnight zone. And they also can't squirt ink.
